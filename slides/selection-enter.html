Selection - Enter

  
    d3.selectAll("circle")
      .data([20,40,35,10])
      .enter()
      .append("circle")
      .attr("cx", function(d,i) {return 50+ 50*i})
      .attr("cy", 50)
      .attr("r", function(d){return d})
      .classed("selected", true)

  • enter() is a selection
  • It's comprised of all new elements
  • Typically used to add new elements
